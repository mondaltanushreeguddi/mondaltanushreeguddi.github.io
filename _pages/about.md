I am a geophysicist working at the intersection of planetary science, geophysical instrumentation, and data analytics. My current research focuses on investigating subsurface scattering structures of the Moon near the Chandrayaan-3 landing site using seismic datasets under an ISRO-funded project at IIT Roorkee.

My broader interests include planetary geophysics, lunar and Martian subsurface exploration, passive seismic methods, artificial intelligence in geoscience, and geophysical instrumentation for space exploration.
